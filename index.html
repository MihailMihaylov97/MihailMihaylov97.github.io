About Me

I am a third year PhD student at the Metaverse Lab, MBZUAI under the supervision of Dr. Hao Li. I specialize in 3D reconstruction and generative AI, with a particular focus on single-view 3D human reconstruction in real time. My research centers on developing innovative algorithms and systems that enable high-quality, real-time 3D representations of humans from minimal input.
